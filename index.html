Puppies

The most paws-itive friend you'll ever have!

Some puppies can weigh 2-3 lb, while larger ones can weigh up to 15-24 lb. A puppy's coat color may change as the puppy grows older, as is commonly seen in breeds such as the Yorkshire Terrier. Puppy refers specifically to young dogs, while "pup" may be used for other animals such as wolves, seals, giraffes, guinea pigs, rats or sharks. A puppy may just be the best thing this planet has evolved. They love everything about you.
